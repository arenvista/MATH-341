# LU Factorization

## Example

Find the LU Factoriazation of:
$$
	A = \begin{bmatrix}
		2 & 2 & 3  \\
		5 & 9 & 10 \\
		4 & 1 & 2 
	\end{bmatrix}
$$

Goal:
$$
	A =
	\begin{array}
		 &\begin{bmatrix}
			   1 & 0 & 0 \\
			   x & 1 & 0 \\
			   x & x & 1
		   \end{bmatrix}
		 &\begin{bmatrix}
			   x & x & x \\
			   0 & x & x \\
			   0 & 0 & x
		   \end{bmatrix}      \\
		 L               &U
	\end{array}
$$
Where $x$ represents any $x \in \mathbb{R}$.

Now row reduct to get $U$. 
- Record the coefficents of each row operation, these are called $k$ values.
